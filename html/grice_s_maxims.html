Grice’s Maxims
Last tended to on May 16, 2022

Maxim of Quantity

Be as informative as possible, but only as informative as needed.

Maxim of Quality

Be truthful / evidence-based.

Maxim of Relation

Be relevant.

Maxim of Manner

Be clear, concise, and orderly.
